Closure within

Closure may mean different things for different people. For me it means distancing myself from the world. I want to let go of everything i am holding because i feel tired. Sometimes i think of giving up everything just to be free. I am happy to have the possibility to express myself through words. Then again it does not make me feel free to just say what i feel. I want sometime for myself doing nothing just being myself.


Another person iknow told me what closure for him was. It is like being inside a jail where you can see the greenery outside but are feeling captured. My hope for an angle remains intact because hope is everything. Have hope and everything might work out eventually. Stay strong because closure within is a difficult subject.



Maybe someone is watching from the windows. Who knows!!
